Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all emails within one to two business days. During busy periods, it may take slightly longer, but we read every message and do our best to reply as soon as possible.

Can I submit a guest post or contribute an article?

We occasionally accept guest contributions from experienced fashion writers and industry professionals. If you are interested in contributing, please send a brief pitch along with links to your previous work to our email address. We will review your proposal and get back to you if it aligns with our editorial standards and content calendar.

Do you accept product samples for review?

We do accept product samples on a case-by-case basis. Please note that receiving a sample does not guarantee a review or a positive mention. All editorial decisions are made independently, and we will always disclose when a product was provided to us free of charge. Send your proposal to our contact email with details about the product and your brand.

How can I report a problem with the website?

If you encounter any technical issues with our website, such as broken pages, display errors, or accessibility concerns, please email us with a description of the problem, the page URL, and the device and browser you are using. This information helps us resolve issues faster.

I have a privacy or data concern. Who should I contact?

For any questions related to your personal data, cookies, or our privacy practices, please email us at contact@trendloopusa.net. You can also review our Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we handle user data.

Please note that TrendLoop USA is a digital publication. We do not have a physical office open to visitors. All communication is handled via email.
